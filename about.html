Hello, Meet thRuby!

thRuby was created by parents, for parents. With the safety of children in mind, thRuby will give parents piece of mind when their children begin to ask for personal freedom. With our products, parents can be sure that they know where their child is when traveling alone, check up on their personal health, keep busy schedules simple and clean, and be sure their child is transported safely to their destination along with games and entertainment for children to keep them happy and engaged. Nothing is more important to us than the relationship between parent and child, therefore we will continue to work our hardest to ensure that parents and children alike will have a seamless, entertaining, and fun-filled experience with our applications.
